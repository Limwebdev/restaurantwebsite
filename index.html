About

  99 茶餐室（Restoran 99）
 restoran99

Restaurant 99 is located at Taman University, Our restaurant provide Signature HAKKA dishes such as: Black Fungus with Fried Pork, HAKKA YONG TAU FU,Black Vinegar Pig Trotter, HAKKA Braised Pork with Taro and many others....
